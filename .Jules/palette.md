## 2024-05-28 - [Accessible Animated Dropdowns]
**Learning:** Custom animated dropdown menus (like those built with Framer Motion) inherently lack the semantic meaning and keyboard interactions provided by native `<select>` or `<details>` elements. A common a11y pitfall is failing to implement Escape key dismissal, outside click detection, and the necessary ARIA attributes (`aria-expanded`, `aria-haspopup`, `role="menu"`, `role="menuitem"`), which leaves keyboard and screen reader users trapped or without context.
**Action:** When building or auditing custom dropdown components, always explicitly bind global `keydown` (for Escape) and `mousedown` (for outside clicks) event listeners while tracking the container via `useRef`. Additionally, verify that the toggle button correctly updates `aria-expanded` and that the dropdown container and its items have proper menu ARIA roles.
