## 2024-05-15 - Accessible Framer Motion Dropdowns
**Learning:** Custom animated dropdowns built with framer-motion often lack native `<select>` accessibility out-of-the-box. We must manually supply `aria-haspopup`, `aria-expanded`, `role="menu"`, and `role="menuitem"` to ensure screen readers can understand the interactive context. Also, keyboard accessibility (like `Escape` to close) and 'click outside' to close are vital for seamless usability. Furthermore, clearing classes with `document.documentElement.className = ''` can cause a `react-hooks/immutability` linting error or throw a `DOMException` error for multi-class strings, it is preferred to use `.setAttribute('class', '')`.
**Action:** Always wrap custom dropdown logic with click-outside hooks, an Escape key listener, and apply full WAI-ARIA `menu` and `menuitem` roles and corresponding attributes.
