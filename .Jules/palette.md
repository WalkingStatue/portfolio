## 2024-05-22 - Escape Key and Click-Outside behavior for Dropdowns
**Learning:** Custom UI dropdown menus (like ThemeSwitcher) frequently lack standard accessible interactions like closing when a user presses 'Escape' or clicks outside the menu. Relying solely on a click-to-toggle button creates a "trapped" feeling for users. Furthermore, without `aria-expanded` and `aria-haspopup="menu"`, screen readers cannot announce the state of the toggle, and missing `role="menu"` or `role="menuitem"` reduces standard menu navigation context.
**Action:** When implementing custom popovers, dropdowns, or modals, always ensure click-outside functionality via a ref and a `mousedown` event listener, close on 'Escape' via a `keydown` listener, and provide corresponding `aria` and `role` attributes to accurately reflect the state to assistive technologies.
